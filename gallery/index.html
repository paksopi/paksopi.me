Projects

SewaTani.com

Oct 2024 – Jul 2025

SewaTani is a web-based platform developed to help small-scale farmers in Malaysia easily rent agricultural machinery. The system centralizes machinery listings, bookings, and management processes for users, staff, and administrators. Designed with a focus on accessibility, cost-efficiency, and operational transparency, SewaTani supports sustainable digital transformation in the agricultural sector. Built using the Agile methodology, the platform integrates user authentication, booking management, and staff administration through a responsive interface and secure database operations.
